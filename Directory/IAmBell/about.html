About: How to use IAmBell

  • What is IAmBell

    • IAmBell is a place where anyone can post about any topic that they please as long as it abinds by thier local law.

    • Here at IAmBell, you can start posting immediately. You do not have to register and you do not need a username.

  • Home

    • The home page is where all the latest new post will be displayed. This is where you can find a post on any topic from any of the topics provided on the website.

    • On this page you will find the articles with the inital comment from the orignal poster. To see the comments of other users on this article then, you must click read more.

  • Post

    • The post page is where you can post new articles on any topic. On this page you will be greeted with a simple box that will ask you to put in the title of the article, you can put a username if you please, and your comment about that topic.

  • Article

    • The article pages are where you can find the discussions on topics. The topic comment is the orginal post and the comments that follow are from other users.

    • To post your own comment you must enter in the ID number of the article which can be found next to the title of the article, a username if you wish, and your comment. After completing that then, you should press the submit button and your comment will be added.
